About Us

At JB Knowledge Park, we are committed to providing quality education in the fields of technology and management. Our campus offers a dynamic environment with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that prepares students for a successful career in today's competitive world.

JB Knowledge Park is affiliated with Maharishi Dayanand University (MDU), which is renowned for its academic excellence and innovation in research and education.

Courses Offered

We offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Engineering, Management, and Technology, aimed at developing industry-ready professionals.

  • B.Tech (All branches)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
  • MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
  • MBA (Master of Business Administration)

Campus

Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, including advanced labs, a library, and recreational spaces, creating a conducive environment for learning and growth. We also have grounds for cricket, football, badminton, basketball, and volleyball, providing ample opportunities for students to engage in sports and extracurricular activities.
